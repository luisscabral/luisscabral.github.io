About Luis

Born and raised in the archipelago of Azores, in Portugal. I studied in Lisbon first, had a 1-year experience in Brazil and more recently followed studies in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. I value an international environment and cross-cultural experiences. At the moment, I am flexible to relocate to multiple destinations in Europe.
My main (professional) interests are Business Intelligence, Data Analysis, Operations, Supply Chain and Project Management. Given my background, I have particular knowledge of the construction and real estate industries, but am open to work in other interesting, dynamic contexts.
